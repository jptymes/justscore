Quick Start
JustScore is free. No download, no account, no catch. Open it in any browser on any device and you're ready to play.
1
Open JustScore

Visit justscore.justintymesports.net in Chrome on your phone, tablet, or Chromebook. Works on any device.

2
Rotate to Landscape

JustScore is designed for landscape orientation. Rotate your device sideways for the full scoreboard experience.

3
Select Your Game

Tap the ☰ menu (top left) and choose a game preset. Mini-Pro is pre-selected — perfect for JustInTymeSports hoops.

4
Tap Start — Play Ball

Tap ▶ Start in the control bar to start both the game clock and shot clock together. Score with the +1, +2, +3 buttons. The shot clock buzzes automatically when time expires.

📲
Install on Your Device
JustScore is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it on your home screen like a native app — no App Store required.
Android / Chrome

Open JustScore in Chrome → tap the ⋮ menu → tap Add to Home Screen → tap Add. The JustScore icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to launch fullscreen.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

Open JustScore in Safari → tap the Share button (□↑) → scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen → tap Add. Launch from your home screen for the full experience.

Chromebook

Open JustScore in Chrome → look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar → click it → click Install. JustScore will appear in your app launcher.

Cast to Your TV

On Android or Chromebook: open JustScore in Chrome → tap the ⋮ menu → tap Cast → select your Chromecast or smart TV. Your phone becomes the controller, the TV becomes the scoreboard. Full gym experience at home.

Updates

JustScore updates automatically in the background. When a new version is ready, you'll see a prompt when you open the app: "A new version of JustScore is available. Update now?" Tap OK to reload with the latest version. Tap Cancel to keep using the current version — you'll be prompted again next time you open the app.

Scoreboard Controls
Game Clock

Use the three buttons above the game clock: ▶ to start (F5), ■ to stop (F6), and ↺ to reset (Ctrl+T). The colon blinks when the clock is running and goes dim when stopped. When time expires the buzzer sounds and the display flashes.

When the clock is stopped, four adjustment buttons appear: −1s, −15s, +15s, +1s. Use these to fine-tune the time. They hide automatically when the clock is running.

Shot Clock

Four buttons control the shot clock: ▶ to start (F7), ■ to stop (F8), ↺ to reset (Ctrl+Y), and ↺▶ to reset and immediately restart (F9 or N). Use ↺▶ on change of possession. The shot clock turns amber when 5 seconds or fewer remain. The buzzer sounds when it expires.

Scoring

Tap +2, +3, or +1 to add points for each team. Tap − to subtract one point if needed. Scores display as two-digit LED numbers.

Fouls & Bonus

Tap + next to the foul count to add a foul. When a team reaches the foul limit for the selected preset, the opposing team's Bonus indicator lights up automatically — they earn bonus free throws. Tap − to remove a foul if entered in error. You can also tap the Bonus light directly to toggle it on or off manually.

Possession Arrows

Tap the arrow indicator (◀ or ▶) next to either team to toggle possession. The active arrow lights up in amber.

Period

Period dots advance automatically at the end of each period. Tap any dot to jump to that period manually. Fouls reset automatically when the period advances.

Control Bar

The bottom row of the scoreboard has five buttons that span the full width: ▶ Start and ■ Stop control both clocks together; ↺ Clock resets both clocks; Period +1 advances to the next period; Full Reset clears all scores, fouls, periods, and clocks back to the start of a new game.

Remote Sync
Share your scoreboard live with any device, anywhere. No shared network required — works across Wi-Fi, cellular, and different networks simultaneously.
Controller (Scorekeeper)

Open the ☰ menu and tap Sync: Start. The game clock display switches to show a 4-digit code in amber. The shot clock counts down the 60-second join window. Read the code to your display device(s). All scoreboard controls are locked during setup except ■ Stop, which closes the join window early once everyone is connected.

Display Device

Open the ☰ menu and tap Sync: Join. Enter the 4-digit code on the numeric pad. The display switches to scoreboard-only view — no controls, no menu. The scoreboard updates live as the controller scores and runs the clock.

When the controller disconnects, the display reverts to normal mode on next load.

Works Everywhere

Controller and display devices do not need to be on the same network. A phone on your home Wi-Fi, a TV on a separate IoT network, and a family member in another state can all connect to the same live scoreboard using just the 4-digit code.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is JustScore really free? ▼
Yes — completely free. No download, no account, no subscription, no ads. JustScore is our way of saying thank you for dreaming. Open it at justscore.justintymesports.net and play.
Does JustScore work offline? ▼
Yes — after your first visit, JustScore is cached by your browser and works without an internet connection. Install it as a PWA (see the Install section above) for the best offline experience.
What devices does JustScore work on? ▼
Any device with a modern browser — Android phones and tablets, iPhones, iPads, Chromebooks, laptops, and desktop computers. JustScore is optimized for landscape orientation on phones and tablets.
Can I cast JustScore to my TV? ▼
Yes — on Android or Chromebook, open JustScore in Chrome and use the Cast option from the browser menu. Select your Chromecast-enabled TV. Your phone controls the scoreboard; the TV displays it. Works great for living room games.
What is the Mini-Pro preset? ▼
Mini-Pro is designed specifically for JustInTymeSports mini basketball hoops — 6-minute periods, 4 periods, 12-second shot clock, and a 3-foul bonus limit. It creates a fast, competitive game that matches the scale of the hoop.
How do I reset everything for a new game? ▼
Tap Full Reset in the bottom control bar. This clears all scores, fouls, periods, and both clocks back to the beginning of a new game with the current preset.
The buzzer sounded — how do I silence it? ▼
The buzzer plays once when the clock expires and stops automatically. Make sure your device volume is turned up to hear it — JustScore uses your device's media volume. You can also mute your device to disable all sounds.
How do I change the period length? ▼
Select a different game preset from the ☰ menu — each preset has a different period length. For a fully custom setup, select the Custom preset which starts at 8 minutes per period.
Can I use JustScore for a real gym or school game? ▼
Absolutely. Cast to a large screen TV or projector for a gym-quality experience. The College and Pro presets match regulation timing. JustScore has been used for mini-pro games, youth leagues, and school events.
What hoop do you recommend for home use? ▼
JustInTymeSports has been making premium indoor mini basketball hoops since 1996 — all Made in the USA with welded steel frames and polycarbonate backboards. Visit justintymesports.net to find the right hoop for your space and budget.
